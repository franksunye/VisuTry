# Business Partner Management Skill

This skill codifies the "Agile & Lean" business philosophy for handling global partnership inquiries. It ensures that every interaction is both professional and strategically recorded in the internal CRM.

## 1. Core Philosophy (Warm Partners, Cold Numbers)
- **Agile & Lean**: Avoid corporate bureaucracy. Be direct and friendly.
- **Strategic Discipline**: Never reveal pricing in the first email. Focus on "Discovery & KYC" first.
- **Product-First**: Always validate "Product-Market Fit" before committing to a distributor structure.

## 2. CRM Maintenance
- **Primary Ledger**: `.private/visutry-business/leads.md`.
- **Golden Rule**: Never reply to a business email without updating the CRM.
- **Status Updates**: Always move the lead through the standard stages (`🟡 待回复` -> `🔵 已发 Stage 1` -> `🟣 已发 Stage 2` -> `🟢 Pilot`).

## 3. Communication Guardrails
> [!DANGER]
> **NO AUTOMATIC EMAILS**: Never send an email to a human partner without showing the final draft to the USER and getting explicit approval.
