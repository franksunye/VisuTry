- Reddit: slow account warmup, 1 short helpful comment per day.
- Style forums: participate naturally first, no product link until trust exists.
- Q&A/search surfaces: answer fully, disclose builder relationship if linking.
- YouTube comments: short helpful comments on relevant eyewear videos, no link unless the creator asks.
