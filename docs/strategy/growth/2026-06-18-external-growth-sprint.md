# VisuTry External Growth Sprint

Date: 2026-06-18
Window: 2026-06-18 to 2026-07-12
Owner: Codex
Status: Active

## Objective

Generate enough qualified external traffic that product decisions are based on real visitors rather than owner testing.

North star for this sprint:

> 200 qualified external sessions that produce at least 20 high-value product actions.

A high-value action is one of:

- face analysis upload
- face analysis completion
- continuation from face analysis to try-on
- glasses try-on start
- pricing view from a commercial landing page
- checkout start or successful unlock

Channel contributions are planning assumptions, not promises. Reallocate effort every seven days toward sources producing qualified sessions or product actions.

## Three Campaign Angles

### 1. Which Glasses Suit My Face?

User problem: face-shape advice is hard to apply to actual frames.

Proof asset:

- `public/assets/marketing/face-analysis-slide-report.png`

Destination:

- `/en/face-analysis`

Message:

- Detect a likely face shape.
- Get frame directions.
- Validate the recommendation with virtual try-on.

### 2. Try Glasses from a Photo or Screenshot

User problem: many store pages have no useful virtual try-on.

Proof assets:

- `public/assets/marketing/compare-slide-woman.png`
- `public/assets/marketing/compare-slide-man.png`

Destination:

- `/en/try-on/glasses`

Message:

- Upload one portrait.
- Use a frame product image or screenshot.
- Generate a shareable visual check before buying.

### 3. Compare Frames Before Choosing

User problem: a single try-on result does not make comparison easy.

Proof asset:

- Existing frame comparison product UI.

Destination:

- `/en/try-on/glasses/compare`

Message:

- Compare several frame directions on the same photo.
- Reduce memory-based switching between product tabs.
- Keep the claim to visual decision support, not fit or prescription accuracy.

## Daily Operating Block

Keep the routine small enough to execute:

1. 15 minutes: check GSC, GA acquisition sources, and replies.
2. 35 minutes: publish or improve one useful X/Reddit placement.
3. 25 minutes: submit or refresh one directory listing.
4. 30 minutes: research and send two personalized outreach messages.
5. 15 minutes: update the queue with URLs, replies, and next action.

Do not spend the daily block on general page polishing unless data identifies a specific bottleneck.

## Measurement

Use campaign links in [Distribution Kit](./2026-06-18-distribution-kit.md).

Minimum placement log fields:

- date
- channel
- live URL
- campaign angle
- UTM source and medium
- external sessions
- high-value actions
- replies or link outcome

Direct traffic is not credited to this sprint unless a unique campaign link or partner placement makes attribution credible.

## Decision Rules

- If a channel produces no qualified visits after three credible placements, pause it.
- If a channel produces at least five qualified visits or one product action, publish a second angle there.
- If a directory requires payment before showing evidence of traffic or fit, skip it during this sprint.
- Treat Product Hunt as a coordinated launch, not one more directory submission.
- On Reddit, lead with a complete answer and disclose the builder relationship. Do not drop the same promotional post across multiple communities.
- Refresh an existing listing instead of creating a duplicate when VisuTry is already indexed.
- If outreach reply rate is below 3% after 30 personalized messages, replace the segment or offer before sending more.
- If visitors arrive but do not start a product action, fix the matching landing page before increasing distribution.
- If a partner requests product work, validate the commercial commitment before building custom integration.

## Guardrails

- Do not publish private user photos or try-on results without explicit consent.
- Use existing public marketing assets until consented case material exists.
- Disclose that VisuTry is the builder when posting in communities.
- Do not invent testimonials, accuracy claims, customer outcomes, or merchant adoption.
- Do not mass-post identical copy or send generic bulk outreach.
